About Our Hospital

Shaafi Hospital is a modern healthcare facility located in Mogadishu, Somalia. We are committed to providing high-quality medical services, advanced diagnostic tools, and compassionate patient care to our community around the clock.

Our medical team consists of experienced doctors, nurses, and specialists who work together to ensure the well-being and safety of every patient who walks through our doors.

Our Core Services

  1. Emergency and Trauma Care (Available 24/7)
  2. General Medicine and Specialized Consultations
  3. Advanced Laboratory and Diagnostic Imaging
  4. Inpatient Wards and Intensive Care Units
  5. Pharmacy and Patient Support Services
